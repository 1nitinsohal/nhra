    ABOUT NHRA

    At Newport Harbor Radiology

    At Newport Harbor Radiology Associates (NHRA), you will receive imaging-based diagnosis and treatment from a very experienced team of radiologists. NHRA radiologists are all board-certified by the American Board of Radiology in general radiology while also possessing extensive subspecialty training.

    Many of our radiologists are involved in cutting-edge clinical research endeavors, providing our patients with access to technologies not available at many other facilities throughout California and beyond.
